Access control testing tool to implement and support the adoption of ReACP - Framework for Reverse-Engineering of AC Policies. The ACMate tool is integrated with BurpSuite to leverage BurpSuite's features, including its proxy, its spider, and its site map. Key features of ACMate include: (1) exploring the web applications to uncover resources, (2) generating the AC test requests and performing AC testing on behalf of various users, and (3) inferring AC policies.
